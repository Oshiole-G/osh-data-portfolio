# Reports & Insights

This folder contains written reports and business insight summaries based on my data analysis projects.

## What You'll Find Here
- 1-page executive summaries
- KPI performance reports
- Business insight write-ups
- Recommendations based on trends and patterns
- Dashboard interpretation notes

## Purpose
The goal of these reports is to demonstrate how I translate raw data into clear business decisions and actionable insights.

## Format
Reports may include:
- PDF summaries
- Markdown reports (README-style)
- Charts and visuals exported from Excel / Power BI
- Supporting tables when needed

## Note
All reports are based on practice datasets or anonymized project scenarios.
